Temi's Special Lasagna

Description

This is a recipe for Temi's special lasagna recipe adapted from an original recipe from her family. It is a quick and easy recipe that brings a bit of the traditional taste of a Brazilian dish mixed to a traditional italian taste that is easy to anyone to make!

Ingredients

  • 1kg of ground beef
  • 1 and 1/2 onion
  • 6 cloves of garlic
  • Salt and Black pepper to taste
  • Paprika
  • Vegetable oil
  • 1 tablespoon of soy sauce
  • 1 tablespoon of fine herbs mix
  • 100g of sliced olives.
  • 1/2 a cup of ketchup
  • 1/2 a cup of honey mustard
  • 300g of tomato sauce
  • 4 table spoon of all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 liter of whole milk
  • 2 tablespoon of butter
  • 1 box of table cream
  • 100g of shredded provolone cheese
  • 300g of sliced mozzarella cheese
  • 300g of sliced ham
  • 1 package of lasagna dough sheets

Steps

  1. Take the 1 full onion and chop it.
  2. Reserve it for the bolognese sauce.
  3. Take the other half onion and chop it.
  4. Reserve it for the bechamel sauce.
  5. Mince the garlic and reserve half of it for the bolognese and the other half for the bechamel sauce.
  6. On a medium sized pan, add a bit of vegetal oil and saute the onions you reserved for the bolognese sauce.
  7. As soon as the onions start to become transparent, add the minced garlic and let it start frying slightly.
  8. Add the ground beef and mix it so it can start cook evenly.
  9. Season it with salt, black pepper, paprika and fine herbs.
  10. Mix it to incorporate all the seasoning and the soy sauce.
  11. Mix it a bit more and close the pan with a lid.
  12. Let it cook in medium-low heat.
  13. While the ground beef is cooking, get another medium sized pan for the bechamel.
  14. Heat the butter on medium flame until it melt and add the onion and garlic.
  15. Add the milk, the flour and a pinch of salt to taste.
  16. Mix everything and keep mixing until it thickens.
  17. Turn the flame to low and let the sauce cook for around 5 minutes.
  18. Turn off the heat and add half of the shredded provolone and the table cream and mix it.
  19. Let it rest.
  20. Check to see if the ground beef is properly cooked and if its water has evaporated.
  21. Add the sliced olives to the ground beef and mix it with everything.
  22. Add the tomato sauce, ketchup and mustard to the ground beef.
  23. Mix it and let it cooked for 3 more minutes.
  24. Let it rest.
  25. Pre-heat the oven for 10 minutes at 180ºC/356ºF.
  26. While the oven is pre-heating, grab a medium to big size baking sheet and let's start layering our lasagna.
  27. Start with a thin layer of our bolognese sauce covering all the sheet's surface.
  28. Follow with a layer of lasagna dough sheets.
  29. Next, add a new layer of bolognese sauce followed by a layer of bechamel sauce.
  30. Added a layer of ham followed by the mozzarella cheese.
  31. Repeat the steps, adding lasagna dough sheets.
  32. Keep repeating the dough and sauces layers until you run out of mozzarella cheese or space, whichever comes first!
  33. Lastly, after the last layer of mozzarella, sprinkle a little bit of the provolone on top of your lasagna.
  34. Cover the baking sheet with aluminum foil and put it into the oven.
  35. Let it bake for 30 minutes.
  36. Take the lasagna out of the oven and take of the foil.
  37. Put it into the oven again and let it bake for 10 more minutes, uncovered.
  38. Turn of the oven and take the lasagna out of the oven.
  39. Let it rest for at least 30 minutes to an hour before serving.
  40. Enjoy!~

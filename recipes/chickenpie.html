Chicken Pie

Description

This is a recipe for a classic Brazilian Chicken Pie made with a Blender. It is a quick and easy recipe that brings a bit of the traditional taste of a Brazilian pie to the table and that can be enjoyed by anyone!

Ingredients

  • 1 cup of vegetable oil
  • 2 cups of all-purpose flour
  • 2 cups of whole milk
  • 3 whole eggs
  • 1 tablespoon of yeast
  • A pinch of salt
  • 50g of shredded parmesan cheese
  • Half an onion chopped
  • 3 cloves of minced garlic
  • 1 tomato chopped
  • 500g of shredded chicken
  • A pinch of chilli flakes
  • A pinch of paprika
  • 1 tablespoon of MSG
  • A pinch of powdered chicken stock
  • 50g of chopped olives
  • Sal and black pepper to taste
  • 3 or 4 tablespoons of requeijão

Steps

  1. Start by adding to the blender the oil, milk and eggs.
  2. Next, follow up with the flour, shredded parmesan cheese, the pinch of salt and yeast.
  3. Blend everything together until fully incorporated.
  4. Let it rest
  5. For the chicken, add the chopped onion with the chilli flakes to a pan with a splash of oil and let it simmer until the onions start to get translucent.
  6. Add the minced garlic and fry it until it starts to get golden.
  7. Add the shredded chicken and mix everything so they can get to know each other well.
  8. Add paprika, chicken stock, salt and pepper and mix everything.
  9. Let it cook for around 3 minutes and add the chopped tomato.
  10. Mix once again and let it cook until done, for around 5 more minutes.
  11. Add the chopped olives and mix with the rest of the filling.
  12. Let it rest.
  13. Prepare a medium sized baking sheet and around 1/3 of the pie mixture to it, covering all of the bottom of the sheet.
  14. Add the Requeijão to the filling and mix it until fully incorporated.
  15. Add the filling to the baking sheet and spread it until fully covering the pie.
  16. Cover the filling with the remaining 2/3 of the pie mixture.
  17. Put the pie in an oven that was preheated at 180ºC/356ºF for at least 10 minutes.
  18. Let the pie bake for at least 40 minutes.
  19. Check if the pie is baked and if done, take it out of the oven and let it rest for at least 30 minutes before serving.
  20. Enjoy!~
